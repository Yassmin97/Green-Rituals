About Us


Welcome to Green Rituals where we focus on creating a relaxing and peaceful environment for our customers. We specialize in professional spa treatments, including relaxing massages and facials. With us you get a tailor-made and personal experience, where each treatment is designed to promote both physical and mental well-being. Explore our world of relaxation and beauty and let us take care of you in the best possible way.

Our Therapists

Rima - Massage Therapist


Rima is an experienced massage therapist known for her intuitive touch and personalized approach. Her main focus lies in various massage modalities, including Swedish, deep tissue, and aromatherapy massages. With a keen understanding of anatomy and a calming demeanor, Rima customizes each session to address clients' specific needs, providing relaxation and relief from muscular tension.

Sophie - Esthetician


Sophie is a skilled esthetician specializing in facial treatments at the spa. With a passion for skincare, she offers a range of facial therapies, from rejuvenating facials to deep-cleansing treatments. Sophie's expertise lies in analyzing skin conditions and recommending tailored skincare regimens. Her meticulous care and attention leave clients with radiant, glowing skin after every session.

Jaz - Spa Specialist


Jaz is the spa's specialist offering a diverse array of spa treatments. Her expertise spans various holistic practices, from body wraps and exfoliations to hydrotherapy. Jaz's dedication to wellness and relaxation helps clients experience a rejuvenating escape. She ensures a serene spa journey, combining her knowledge of therapies to create an immersive and revitalizing experience.
